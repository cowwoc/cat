## Turn 1

You are writing a WHY paragraph for a new prohibition rule in a skill. The prohibition is:

> BLOCKED: Do not edit the config file while the service is running.

You have drafted this WHY paragraph:

> This is required. The config is critical.

Evaluate whether this WHY paragraph meets the requirements of skill-conventions.md § The Four-Component
Structure for WHY paragraphs.

## Assertions

1. response must identify that the WHY paragraph is invalid because it uses "This is required"
   instead of explaining what happens if the rule is violated
2. response must identify that the paragraph lacks a required causal signal word: "because",
   "otherwise", "this prevents", "this ensures", or "without this"
3. response must provide a corrected WHY paragraph that includes at least one of the required
   causal signal words and explains the actual consequence of violation
4. response must note the 1–3 sentence length constraint for WHY paragraphs
